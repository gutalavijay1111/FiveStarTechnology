Frequently Asked Questions

What is Shopify?

Shopify is a commerce platform that allows anyone to set up an online store and sell their products. Shopify is our cheaper option of eCommerce that we offer here at FiveStar Technologies, as opposed to using Craft Commerce.

Why choose FiveStar to develop my Shopify Website?

Our team of Shopify experts help people build an online store with hundreds of built-in features, and has the ability to integrate with third-party apps. We can take care of everything for you, so you don't have to worry about it. We pay close attention to every detail, so nothing is missed from a design and development perspective.

Why choose Shopify?

We can build a fully functional custom Shopify eCommerce store within just 4 weeks. Integrated with features you need to start, run, and grow your business, it is the cheaper eCommerce option we offer here at Shape.

What are the USP’s of Shopify?

  • Shopify makes it easy to build and manage your online store.
  • Use one platform to sell products to anyone, anywhere.
  • Take the guesswork out of marketing with built-in tools that help you analyse campaigns on Facebook and Google.
  • Use a single dashboard to manage orders, shipping, and payments anywhere you go.

Why should I use Shopify over more known platforms?

Here at Shape, we use both Shopify and Craft Commerce. Shopify is a no-fuss platform that is easy to use and allows for a faster turnaround so you can be up and running fairly quickly. We wrote an article on the pros and cons of Shopify vs Craft Commerce if you want to know more.

Can you show me some Shopify websites you've built?

Sure, we've worked on a range of shopify website projects such as Riley Studio, Killer Impression, Repentance x Smile, Idyll Home, Gallant Sports, Bionix Sports and Pale Fox Prosecco just to name a few.
